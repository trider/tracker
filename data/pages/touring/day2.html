Ascend Mt. Carmel for a beautiful outlook from the Muhraka, traditional site of the dramatic confrontation between Elijaha and the prophets of Ba’al and enjoy the incredible view from the rooftop of the Carmelite Monastery.

Begin today’s ride at Kibbutz Ramat Ha'Shofet. Ride through an area known as “the Tuscany of the Middle East". In 2011 UNESCO recognized the 20,000 acres of the Ramot Menashe district as a biosphere for preservation, in order to maintain the natural and rural characteristics of this area!

Ride toward Megiddo Junction, passing the famous archeological site of Tel Megiddo, known in Christian tradition as Armageddon site of humanity’s last great battle. Continue across the fertile fields of the Jezre'el Valley. Today’s cycling ends at Givat HaMoreh, a hilll mentioned in the Book of Judges. From this point we will drive to the beautiful cliff of Mt. Arbel overlooking the Sea of Galilee. We will enjoy a short hike, with magnificent views the Sea of Galilee, the Golan Heights and Mount Hermon.
