Begin riding at Mini Israel, near the Latrun Junction.Cycle towards the Latrun Monastery, a Trappist Monastery, whose residents live their lives based on the values of simplicity, harmony with nature and keeping strict vows of silence!

Enjoy the open views along Road no. 3 until the Nachshon Junction. Here, we will start riding on the curving "Heroes Road", named after two local Biblical heroes, Nachson and Shimshon.

Finish riding at the Eshtaol Junction and travel by bus to the nearby Avshalom Reserve and its famous Stalactite Cave, ranked as one of the best caves in the world, as far as the variety and beauty of the stalactites. From the Kerem Intersection at the bottom of the picturesque village of Ein Karem, join the rest of the WOL riders, and ride together up into Jerusalem!
