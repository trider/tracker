Participate in the festive Opening Ceremony of the 14th Wheels of Love Ride Gather at the Mevo'ot Yam Harbor in Michmoret! Begin riding south to Beit Yanai, crossing the Nachal Alexander estuary to the Mediterranean. Say farewell to the coast and ride eastwards through Moshav Kfar Vitkin. The first part of the ride takes us through the beautiful groves and orchards of Emek Hefer. Ride past the Ruppin Academic Center and continue south to road 57, where we turn to the east. From here we’ll ride north for the rest of the day. Pass Kibbutz Yad Hannah, named after Hannah Senesh one of the 37 Jews who parachuted during World War II into Hungary, and continue to Bat Hefer.Today’s cycling ends at Kibbutz Ein Shemer.

Visit the Kibbutz Old Courtyard Museum for a fun hands-on experience which takes us back in time to the early days of the Kibbutz. Travel to the Solar Garden in Binyamina and learn about this unique initiative designed to promote green energy sources and new environmental technologies. Time and weather permitting, visit the archeological site of Mei Kedem, an ancient water system built during the Roman and Byzantine periods.
